The What & Why of Yoga


The word ‘yoga’ itself came from the Sanskrit word ‘Yui’ meaning ‘to unite’ or ‘to yolk’.
The ancient practice helps to create a deeper connection with ourselves and our surroundings.
With practice, yoga can give us a sense of peace, wellbeing and connection.

Utilising this ancient and complex practice, yoga strives to unite and balance the body, mind and spirit.
It can empower us to achieve greater flexibility, physical strength and stillness of the mind.

Why FlowYoga?

We believe that yoga is for everyone.

Our goal is to make you feel comfortable on your mat and we hope that you’ll embrace yoga off the mat too. We are confident that our classes will change the way you look at your health and your lifestyle.
